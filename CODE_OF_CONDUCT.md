# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation harassment-free for everyone.

## Standards

- Welcoming and inclusive language
- Respectful of differing viewpoints
- Graceful acceptance of constructive criticism

## Enforcement

Violations may be reported to project maintainers.

Adapted from [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org) v2.1.
